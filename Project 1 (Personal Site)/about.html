Education

High School Diploma

I grew up in a small town in Wyoming called Gillette. I graduated from Campbell County High School in 2013... and yes our mascot was a camel.

Associate's Degree

I attended a small community college in my home town of Gillete, Wyoming. There I was a student-athlete and ran cross-country and track and field. I graduated in 2015 with an Associate's of Science in General Studies.

Bachelor's Degree

After graduating from my community college, I moved to Laramie, Wyoming to attend the University of Wyoming. I pursued a Bachelor's of Science in Kinesiology and Health Promotion while working full time in a couple restaurants. I graduated in May 2020, and had anticipated on attending grad-school for occupational therapy after taking a year off to work.

FullStack Academy

After working for a couple years in healthcare and deciding that 2 to 3 more years of schoool sounded unappealing, I decided to change my career trajectory. I began looking into coding in October 2021. After a couple months of self-teaching, I decided I needed a more professional and structured education, so I began looking into bootcamps. After a lot of research, I decided to apply to FullStack Academy and enrolled in their February 2022 cohort!
